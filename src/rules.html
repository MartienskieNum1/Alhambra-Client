Rules

Object of the game

Players who have the most building tiles of each kind in their Alhambra in the scoring rounds are awarded points, the exact amount depending on the type of buildings. Players also receive points for the longest part of wall they have built around their Alhambra.

The winner is the player with the most points at the end.

Getting ready to play

  • Each player is given a starting tile.
  • There are 54 buildings ready for the start of play.
  • The building market is in the middle of the game and the scoring board at the left corner.
  • Each player has a reserve board .
  • There are two scoring cards that can be taken from the pack of money cards.
  • Each player is now given their starting money. You get money until the total is 20 or more.
  • Player with the fewest cards starts; if two players are equal the player with the lowest amount of money starts.
  • There are 4 money cards laid in the middle of the screen.

How to play

Play proceed in a clockwise direction, beginning with the starting player.

The player whose turn it must do one of three things:

  1. Take some money
  2. Buy and position a building tile
  3. Redesign their own Alhambra

After a player's turn is over any money cards and building tiles which have been removed are replaced with new ones so that there are always 4 of each.

Take some money

A player can take any of the money cards from the several money cards if they do not add up to more than 5.

Buy and position a building tile

Buying the tiles

A player can buy a building tile from the market. He must pay at least the price shown on the tile and use the currency displayed next to the tile in the building market. No change is given!!

If you can pay the exact amount it is still your turn and you can decide once more which of the 3 options you would like to take.

While it's your turn no new tiles are placed on the market.

Positioning the tiles

You can place your building into the reserve board or add it to your Alhambra.

Rules for building your Alhambra

You must build your Alhambra according to the following rules:

  • All building tiles must be the same way around as your starting file.
  • Adjoining sides must be the same.
  • You must be able to reach each new building tile "on foot" from the starting tile without crossing a wall.
  • You must not leave any spaces.
